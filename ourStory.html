Our story

“We work, we sweat, so that every kid has a fighting chance”

Hi there!

We are TeeRead, a team of passionate people dedicated to make the reading crisis a problem of the past. I’m Trini, TeeRead’s founder and CEO.

TeeRead was born in Chicago, IL after I learned something that stopped me cold— 65% of our kids in America can’t read proficiently. This means that 65% of our kids can’t learn independently or properly explore and understand new ideas.

I couldn’t believe it; how can children even hope for a bright future if they can’t read proficiently?

Pledging to do something about this, I consulted with teachers across the country to determine how I could help.

Teachers showed me the path

Having been a teacher assistant at a local school during college, I knew that the real changemakers are the women and men showing up in classrooms every day to teach our children.

The frustrations of the teachers I spoke with were clear.

They told me they would be much more effective if they could continually tailor instruction by individually testing each student’s reading fluency and comprehension.

But this would require an extra full day of work every week—impossible! They were already working overtime.

And I thought… wait a second. If Alexa can listen to my voice, create a to-do list, and buy my groceries, why can’t I use the same core AI and speech recognition technology to help those shaping our kids’ futures? Why can’t I make such technology accessible at scale all around the world to end the literacy crisis?

The frustrations of the teachers I spoke with were clear.

Our point of view

We dream of a world in which all children can become whomever they want to be, regardless of their background. We know that besides fulfilling their basic human needs, all children must develop their minds, growing into adults who are free to choose and create the lives that they envision.

This journey starts with reading.

Reading is the stepping-stone to learning; it’s the key to opening possibilities, allowing our children to dream a bright future and believe they can make it happen.

At TeeRead, we sweat and fight to create a world in which every child can read, giving all children a fighting chance to reach their potential and freely choose their future.

This isn’t just a dream; it’s our objective. Let’s make the literacy crisis a problem of the past, because for us every kid counts.
