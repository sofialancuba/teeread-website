What is TeeRead?

TeeRead is an innovative reading platform that automatically assesses students’ reading fluency and comprehension, while also providing students with a library of books personalized at each student’s reading level.

What benefit would I get from using TeeRead?

Not only will you get access to students’ reading fluency and comprehension reports, showing metrics such as WCPM, WPM, and miscue analysis, but you can also assign reading practice from our library of books leveled to each student’s reading ability. Other cool features that you will find in TeeRead are the ability to group your students, edit reports and see the books that your students are loving.

How does TeeRead work?

TeeRead uses a combination of speech recognition and literacy best practices to create the reports that you will see for your students and to match them with the books that they should be reading. We are not re-inventing the wheel; we are just doing what you wished you had time to do, using the latest technology on AI and speech recognition.

What information can I get from TeeRead?

You will get reports showcasing the major fluency and comprehension metrics, including:
  • Reading grade level
  • Words per minute
  • Accuracy rate
  • Words correct per minute
  • Reading comprehension questions
  • Passage's Lexile level
