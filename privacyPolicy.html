TeeRead - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1st, 2021

Thank you for using TeeRead LLC (“TeeRead”, “we”, “us” or “our”). This is the privacy policy (“Policy”) that governs how we use Personal Information (defined below) that we collect, receive and store about you in connection with the use of our website, teeread.com, as well as any other website or mobile application(s) that we may operate (collectively and together with any subdomains and the content and services therein, the “Site”), and the TeeRead automated literacy assessment and reading comprehension platforms (each, a “Service”). The Site and anyService are individually and collectively referred to herein as the “Services”. Certain of our Services are used by children and section three herein, entitled “Children’s Privacy”, detail show we collect, use and store children’s Personal Information in connection therewith.

We value and respect the privacy of our users and protecting your information is important to us. This Policy explains how we handle the collection and use of Personal Information that we obtain and the choices that you can make about how Personal Information is used by TeeRead. “Personal Information” means any information that can be used, alone or in combination with other information, to personally identify an individual, including a first and last name, email address, or a personal profile. In some cases, IP addresses may also be considered Personal Information. Certain clauses of this Policy detail specific pieces of Personal Information that may be collected from time to time – for instance, we explicitly set forth each piece of Personal Information that is collected from children – but for easy comprehension and reference, the generic “Personal Information” is used throughout.

Consent and Modification

We encourage you to review this Policy periodically, as your use of our Services constitutes consent to this Policy. We may periodically update this Policy, and any changes will be reflected here. If the changes are significant, we may provide a more prominent notice, and at our discretion may email you directly with information on the same. If you do not agree with the practices described in this Policy, you should not use our Services or otherwise interact with TeeRead.

If you have any questions about this Policy or how we use your Personal Information and similar data, please feel free to contact us at trini@teeread.com.

Children’s Privacy

TeeRead’s Services include applications and tools that are meant to facilitate ongoing adolescent education. We do not knowingly collect any Personal Information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If we learn that a child under the age of 13 has provided us with Personal Information absent parental consent, or that such Personal Information has otherwise been inadvertently collected, we will delete it in accordance with applicable law, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act.

When we do collect Personal Information from a child under the age of 13, we do so with the consent of the child’s parent, guardian, or another person with such authority (collectively, the child’s “Parent”). By consenting to the collection of a child’s Personal Information as a Parent, you:

  • Represent and warrant that you have the legal authority to provide consent for and act on behalf of the child;
  • Authorize TeeRead to use and otherwise process the Personal Information of the child in accordance with this Policy; and
  • Agree to indemnify and hold TeeRead harmless against any misuse of our Services by the child, or any other third party

Children’s Personal Information We Collect and How We Collect It

Our Services collect the following Personal Information from children: first and last name; class (including a child’s teacher(s)); school name and location; and audio files to assess the child’s reading fluency and comprehension. Based on this information, we assign unique, alphanumeric codes for each individual child. Except for the individual child codes, which are autogenerated by TeeRead, all Personal Information is collected through the Services, either through direct input by the parent or teacher, or directly from the child, such as the audio files.

We only collect children’s Personal Information as is reasonably necessary to appropriately provide our Services. We only retain children’s Personal Information for as long as is necessary to provide our Services. When it is no longer necessary, such as when the Services are concluded, the Personal Information is deleted.

How We Use Children’s Personal Information

We use children’s Personal Information to create individualized reports on reading comprehension and ability. Based on the reports, we recommend a personalized library of books for each child. We do not use children’s Personal Information for any other purpose.

Sharing Children’s Personal Information

We share the Personal Information of children that we collect with teachers, parents, schools and school districts, as applicable. We also share it with our third-party service providers as necessary to provide TeeRead and the Services, each as further set forth below.

TeeRead does not make children’s Personal Information publicly available, either directly or by allowing children to do the same.

Your Choices & Accuracy of Personal Information We Collect

As a Parent, you always have the right to withhold your consent and prevent TeeRead from collecting Personal Information on or from your child. You may also review what Personal Information we have collected on or from your child at any time upon request. You can instruct TeeRead to delete your child’s Personal Information at any time, or withdraw your previously given consent for the further collection or use of your child’s Personal Information.

Likewise, you always have the choice whether or not to provide any other Personal Information to TeeRead. As indicated above, some of our Services require that you register with TeeRead but doing so is at your discretion. If you choose not to provide required Personal Information, you can still use any portion of our Services that do not require Personal Information.

If you do provide us with Personal Information, we want to make sure it remains accurate and up to date. You can use the tools that we make available through our Services to modify or remove certain information related to your Account. If you need to correct or update any other part(s) of your child’s or your own Personal Information, you can submit a change request directly to TeeRead by emailing us at trini@teeread.com. Please be sure to include sufficient details to allow us to make the necessary changes.

What Other Personal Information We Collect and How We Collect It

In order to use the Services that we provide, you may need to use an account (“Account”). Establishing an Account requires only an email address and a password initially, though we do request that you provide supplementary Account information so that we can more effectively provide the Services. An Account is not required, nor do we require that you provide any Personal Information, in order to access the general information on our Site.

If you provide any additional Personal Information beyond what we require, such as in your communications with us, doing so is entirely your choice. We will retain the Personal Information that you choose to provide in those instances.

We also collect certain data about you automatically when you visit our Site or use our Services. This data may include your IP address, the time and date of your visit, your location, the pages and other content that you access, and the number of times you return to the Site. Collection maybe performed either by TeeRead's own software and tools or through our integrations with technology provided by third parties. We collect this information by using technologies such as:

Log Files: A log file is a text file which resides on our servers, and which records pertinent information about your interactions with our Services at the time of access. Log file data is used primarily for diagnostic and auditing purposes.

Cookies: A cookie is a small data file which resides on your device, and which allows us to recognize you as a user when you return using the same device or web browser. Cookie data is used to customize your experience when you use our Site. You can configure your web browser to warn you each time a cookie is being sent or to refuse cookies completely. However, if you opt to refuse cookies created by our Site, you accept that your ability to use our Services may be impaired or limited, up to and including loss of access to certain Services, until such time as you accept these cookies.

Embedded Scripts: An embedded script is programming code (particularly JavaScript) that collects information about your interactions with our Site. The code is temporarily downloaded onto your device, from our server or a third-party service provider, and is deactivated or deleted when you disconnect from our Site. You can configure your web browser to disable the use of embedded scripts. However, if you opt to disable embedded scripts which we incorporate in toour Site, you accept that your ability to use our Services may be impaired or limited, up to and including loss of access to certain Services, until such time as you enable these embedded scripts

We may also use other technologies that collect similar information for security and fraud detection purposes.

How We Use Personal Information

TeeRead uses children’s Personal Information strictly in accordance with the descriptions for the same under the section titled “Children’s Privacy”. Otherwise, we use Personal Information primarily to provide our Services in manners including, but not limited to, responding to inquiries, providing customer service, and sending administrative information. We may use Personal Information to contact you in the future to tell you about new service offerings, promotions, opportunities, or other general information about TeeRead or our Services that we think you might be interested in.

We also use the Personal Information we collect to improve our Site. We review information to learn about how our Site is used and accessed, and to analyze user behavior as a measure of interest in and use of our Site. We may disclose such analyses to third parties in the form of aggregate data, such as overall patterns or demographic reports that do not describe or identify any individual user. We may also use certain subsets of the same information to investigate and prosecute potential breaches of TeeRead license agreements and security policies. We also may share pertinent Personal Information with our contractors, vendors, and other affiliates and partners for the authorized purposes of processing data for marketing, research, analytics, customer support and account management. We use the following third party service providers:

  • We use Amazon Web Services for cloud storage and analytics services;
  • We use MongoDB as a database system;
  • We use Soapbox System API, a speech recognition API, to process audio recordings. All collected audio recordings are anonymized before we share them with Soapbox;
  • We use Canva to provide weekly awards to individual children, broken down by class, school, and/or school district; and
  • We use Tableaux for development and analytics services.

Though it is not often necessary, we reserve the right to disclose Personal Information to third parties to protect the rights, property, or safety of TeeRead, our employees, or others.

Other Disclosures of Personal Information

We do not sell, trade, share, or rent any Personal Information to unaffiliated third parties.

We will not disclose your Personal Information to any third party except as necessary for a legitimate use as described in this Policy, in connection with a bona fide legal dispute (where the information is relevant and in response to a valid, compulsory legal process), or as otherwise required by law.

In the event of a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or other sale or disposition of all or a portion of TeeRead’s assets, we may disclose Personal Information to the acquiring party(s). In that case, the successor’s use of your Personal Information will continue to be subject to this Policy unless a court orders otherwise or the successor notifies you that your Personal Information will be subject to their own privacy policy. If the successor sends you such a notification, it will include an opportunity for you to opt out. If you submit any Personal Information after such a transfer, that information will be subject to the successor’s privacy policy.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these Third Party Services, and we encourage you to read the terms and conditions and privacy policy of any such Third Party Service that you choose to use.

Information Security

TeeRead uses commercially reasonable, industry standard security and backup measures to protect the information submitted to us. No method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage and security is 100% secure, though. While we always strive to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. We encourage all our users to retain copies of all uploaded information on their own systems, to adhere to all corporate security policies or guidelines pertinent to your situation, and to practice personal habits of digital security.
